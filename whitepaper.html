1. Executive Overview

Whale Protocol Official (WPO) is a blockchain-based reporting and market integrity platform designed to detect suspicious on-chain activity, protect investors, and promote transparency.

2. The Problem

  • Increasing scams and fraudulent smart contracts
  • Lack of structured on-chain reporting systems
  • No incentive-driven verification mechanism
  • Weak coordination with blockchain security entities

3. The Solution

WPO provides a decentralized reporting platform where users submit blockchain-related reports through a token-based mechanism ensuring seriousness and preventing spam.

Platform Features

  • Report submission with token staking or instant fee
  • On-chain evidence transparency
  • Public categorized reports
  • Security-focused analysis publication

4. WPO Tokenomics

Token Name: Whale Protocol Official

Symbol: WPO

Total Supply: 7,500,000 WPO

Burn Mechanism: None

Locked Supply: 40% of total supply

Token Utility

  • Submitting reports via staking or instant token fee
  • Access to premium analytical tools
  • Governance participation (future phase)
  • Platform operational support

Report Submission Mechanism

Users must either stake WPO tokens temporarily or pay a direct WPO fee to submit a report. This ensures report seriousness and reduces spam or malicious submissions.

5. Governance & Platform Coordination

The platform may coordinate with blockchain explorers, security communities, and network administrators when serious violations are detected.

WPO governance mechanisms may evolve to include token-holder voting for platform updates and policy changes.

6. Security & Integrity

  • No financial advice provided
  • Evidence-based reporting only
  • Transparent publication model
  • Cooperation with ecosystem security entities when required

7. Disclaimer

Whale Protocol Official (WPO) does not provide financial advice, investment guarantees, or price predictions. The token is a utility asset within the ecosystem. Users are responsible for their own decisions and due diligence.
