1. Executive Summary

Whale Protocol Official (WPO) is an independent community initiative dedicated to strengthening integrity and transparency within digital asset markets through structured reporting, evidence-based analysis, and responsible risk alerts.

As fraud, rug pulls, coordinated manipulation, and misleading promotional practices continue to increase, WPO provides a technical and operational framework enabling the community to report suspicious activities in an organized, documented, and non-defamatory manner. WPO does not provide financial advice or price predictions.

2. Market Problem

  • Fraudulent or misleading projects
  • Wallets linked to scam activity
  • Coordinated price manipulation
  • Unlocked liquidity risks
  • Dangerous smart contract privileges
  • Centralized or unfair token distributions
  • Retail investors often lack advanced analytical tools

3. Mission & Vision

Mission

Build a transparent, evidence-based reporting system that reduces risk and supports investor protection.

Vision

Become a recognized integrity layer that strengthens digital markets through transparency and community accountability.

5. How the Protocol Works

Step 1 – Report Submission

Users submit reports supported by TX hashes, wallet addresses, explorer links, and timeline descriptions.

Step 2 – Evidence Review

Analyze liquidity status, contract ownership, token distribution, and suspicious transaction patterns.

Step 3 – Risk Classification

  • HIGH – Elevated risk indicators
  • MEDIUM – Activity requiring further verification
  • INFO – General safety reminder

Step 4 – Public Alert

Publish neutral, evidence-based reports without personal accusations or defamatory claims.

Coordinated Action Framework

Documented case files may include transaction analysis, timeline, risk assessment, fund flow mapping, and communication with exchanges or blockchain networks. WPO does not possess enforcement authority.
